# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 9, 2026

## Privacy Overview

Stronglift 5x5 is a StrongLifts 5×5 workout tracking application designed with privacy in mind.

The app does **not collect, store, process, transmit, or share any personal data** with the developer or any third party.

All workout data remains on your device and is under your control.

---

## Information We Collect

We do **not** collect any of the following:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical address
- Date of birth
- Government identification information
- Payment information
- Contacts
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Advertising identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Health information
- Fitness information transmitted to external servers
- Photos, videos, or audio recordings
- Any other personally identifiable information

No user account or registration is required to use the app.

---

## Workout Data

The workout information you enter into the app, including but not limited to:

- Exercise records
- Weights
- Sets
- Repetitions
- Workout history
- Training preferences

is stored locally on your device only.

The developer cannot access, view, retrieve, or share this information.

---

## Data Sharing

The app does **not** share any data with:

- The developer
- Analytics providers
- Advertising networks
- Data brokers
- Marketing platforms
- Government agencies
- Other third parties

No information leaves your device through the app.

---

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services that collect user data, including:

- Analytics services
- Advertising services
- User tracking services
- Cloud synchronization services
- Social media integrations

---

## Internet Access

If the app requests internet access, it is not used to collect or transmit personal data.

The developer does not operate any backend servers that receive user workout information or personal information from the app.

---

## Data Security

Because user data remains stored locally on the device, users maintain control over their information.

Users are responsible for securing access to their own devices through passwords, biometrics, or other security measures provided by their device manufacturer.

---

## Data Retention

The developer does not retain any user data because no user data is collected.

Data stored locally on the device remains there until it is deleted by the user or removed as part of uninstalling the application.

---

## Children's Privacy

This application is intended for a general audience.

The app does not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under the age of 13 or any other age.

Since no personal information is collected from any users, no special processing of children's data occurs.
